# Rapido Ride Booking Analytics Interactive Dashboard

## Project Objective
The main objective of this project is to understand how ride bookings are performing by analyzing real data. It focuses on identifying patterns in bookings, revenue, and cancellations to see what is working well and where improvements are needed.

By using tools like Excel, SQL, and Power BI, this project aims to turn raw data into clear and useful insights that can help make better decisions. The goal is not just to analyze numbers, but to understand the story behind them and find ways to improve customer experience and overall service performance.

## Questions (Dashboard Requirements)
- What is the total number of bookings and total revenue generated in the given time period.
- What percentage of bookings were successful compared to cancelled and incomplete rides.
- Which booking status contributes the highest to overall revenue.
- How does ride demand vary over time? Are there any noticeable peak days.
- Which vehicle type generates the highest booking value and revenue.
- Is there a significant difference in performance across different vehicle categories.
- What is the overall cancellation rate, and how does it impact revenue.
- What are the main reasons for ride cancellations from the customer side.
- What are the major reasons for cancellations from the driver side.
- How do customer and driver ratings compare across different vehicle types.
- Are ratings consistent, or do certain vehicle types have better performance.
- Who are the top customers contributing to the highest booking value.
- How does revenue vary by booking status (success vs cancelled).
- What insights can be drawn from daily revenue trends.

## Process
- Defined the goal of the project — to analyze ride bookings, revenue, cancellations, and overall performance.
- Gathered the dataset containing booking details, vehicle types, revenue, and ratings.
- Cleaned the data using Excel and Power Query by handling missing values, removing duplicates, and standardizing formats.
- Processed the data by creating required columns and calculating key metrics like total bookings, revenue, and cancellation rate.
- Imported the cleaned dataset into Power BI for visualization and analysis.
- Created charts, KPIs, and visuals such as bar charts, pie charts, and line graphs to represent insights clearly.
- Used slicers and filters to allow dynamic analysis based on date, vehicle type, and booking status.
- Analyzed the visuals to identify trends, patterns, and key business insights.

## Dashboard Insights
- The platform recorded 100K total bookings, showing strong and consistent demand.
- Around 63% of bookings were successful, contributing the majority of total revenue (~28M), making ride completion a key driver of business performance.
- The cancellation rate is 27%, which is relatively high and indicates operational inefficiencies and potential revenue loss.

## Conclusion
Looking at the dashboard, it’s clear that the platform is doing well — there are a large number of bookings, good revenue, and overall positive ratings from users. This shows that people are actively using and trusting the service.

At the same time, one thing that stands out is the high number of cancellations. It feels like there’s a gap between booking a ride and actually completing it. Fixing this gap can make a big difference, not just in revenue but also in user experience.

In the end, the business has a strong base, and with some improvements in reducing cancellations and managing rides better, it has great potential to grow even further.
